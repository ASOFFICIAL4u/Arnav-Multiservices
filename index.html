About Us

Maharashtra's Trusted Government Service Partner

Arnav Multiservices is a Maharashtra Government Approved Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) center, proudly operating as both an Aaple Sarkar Kendra and a registered Common Service Centre (CSC). We bring essential government services directly to the people of our community.

Led by Vijay Sirsathe, our center is built on three pillars: speed, transparency, and trust. Whether you need a caste certificate, PAN card, driving license, or any other government document, we handle it with professionalism and care — so you don't have to navigate complex government portals alone.
